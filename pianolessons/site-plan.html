Home page

Welcome to Grigg Studios, where we teach kids the wonder of making music. Our studio is located in the center of our thriving community her in gorgeous Kuna Idaho. We teach piano lessons to kids all the way from their first year of elementary school through their last year of High School with a passion for making musicians who are ready to go out and rock the world. Develop your talents and learn a new skill as you play piano. Join us and take the journey from beginner to virtuoso.

About Us

Grigg studios started as a small family company back in 2020. As someone who had taken piano lessons for years I am so grateful that my mom decided to give me the gift of piano lessons. It was hard at times, but I'll never forgot all the years I had driving with my brother to piano lessons each work and complaining about practicing, but really I always enjoyed the thrill of gliding my fingers along the keys, making the strings sing their beautiful music. As I grew older and became a parent myself, I wanted the same thing for my kids, so I started to teach them lessons. As I watched my children go from playing a faltering "Twinkle Twinkle Little Star" to playing Level 1 and Level 2 pieces in no time, it wasn't long before I wanted to share this with kids everywhere. And so I bought a beautiful new piano that would make any kid's eyes shine with wonder and then I set out to share my gift with the children of the world, starting with my little neighborhood. I started by teaching kids in our little community, including my neighbors kids and their excitement was contagious. Pretty soon we were taking kids from all around town. Now after three years, my wife and I teach more than 30 kids. We love teaching kids of all ages all the way from 1st grade to their 12th grade graduation. If you think piano might be for you or your kids, schedule a lesson to experience the joy of music. We hope to see you soon!

Take Lessons

Piano lessons are offered both individually, and in groups. Your child will have the option to learn with peers, or in a custom one on one lesson with our teachers. Individual Lessons: Individual lessons are great for all ages and they provide an emphasis in preparing solo pieces to play in family recitals or in our annual festival. Students can work towards their trophy cups by earning points through theory tests, festival pieces and concertos. As students progress in their courses they will learn the principles of hand technique, music theory, overall confidence in the instrument. Students will develop a strong musical foundation, and they'll learn a variety of their favorite songs while they play! Half hour and hour long classes are offered weekly. Group Lessons: During group lessons kids will be part of groups according to age and ability. Students who enroll in group lessons will learn the precious skills needed to play the piano while also benefiting from the ear training, sight reading, and rhythym skills that come with playing in a group. Students will also progress rapidly as they play along side their friends in challenges like note naming and scale sequencing. Classes are offered every week for one hour. Pricing Individual lessons: Focus is on learning solo pieces and strengthening individual technique and theory. 30 minute lessons: $15 / lesson 45 minute lessons: $20 / lesson 60 minute lessons: $30 / lesson Group lesson: Focus is on conquering theory challenges as a team, strengthening sight reading and learning to play as part of an ensemble. 60 minute lessons: $20 / lesson Lesson Times Students are welcome to enroll in both individual and group lessons. Individual lessons will be scheduled throughout the week [Mondays through Fridays]. Students may enroll for any available time slot throughout the week. Group lessons will be provided throughout the day Wednesdays and Saturdays.
