Welcome to Grigg Studios, where we teach kids the wonder of making music. Our studio is located in the center of our thriving community her in gorgeous Kuna Idaho. We teach piano lessons to kids all the way from their first year of elementary school through their last year of High School with a passion for making musicians who are ready to go out and rock the world. Develop your talents and learn a new skill as you play piano. Join us and take the journey from beginner to virtuoso.
